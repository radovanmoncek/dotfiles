\title{Anki shared decks}
\author{Radovan Moncek}
\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
Flash cards are a learning method that has become a staple of the modern learning community. It consists of two parts:
front,
and back.
The front part contains a term one is supposed to mentally explain. The back part, on the other hand, houses an explanation of the term of the front.
Anki is a proprietary software that facilitates the creation, editing, and studying flash cards.
\end{abstract}
